About Her

Dr. Brené Brown is a research professor who has spent the past two decades studying courage, vulnerability, shame, empathy. and having authored seven books including five New York Times best-sellers, Brown hosts the Unlocking Us podcast, and her TED talk: "The Power of Vulnerability", has been widely viewed. Her filmed lecture, Brené Brown: The Call to Courage, debuted on Netflix in 2019.
